About EduFort Technologies

EduFort Technologies is a cutting-edge security and attendance monitoring provider, specializing in school safety. With advanced technologies like facial recognition, biometrics, UHF gates, and RFID, we ensure real-time SMS alerts to parents on students' entry, exit, and absenteeism.

  • Real-time Alerts
    Parents receive SMS on entry, exit and absenteeism.
  • Reliable Hardware
    Face recognition, biometric attendance & UHF RFID gates.
  • Admin & Parent Dashboards
    Transparent monitoring and reporting.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the most trusted partner in school safety and student tracking across India and beyond.

Mission:

  1. Provide real-time, secure monitoring solutions for schools.
  2. Leverage modern technologies to enhance student safety.
  3. Build transparency between institutions and parents.
